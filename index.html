Comparison

One-time session vs. maintenance plan

There is no wrong answer — there is one that works better for you.

One-time session

Just once

When you need it, when you can.

  • ✓ Immediate relief from pain or built-up tension
  • ✓ Perfect for getting ready before an important event
  • ✓ No long-term commitment
  • – Your body may return to tension within days
  • – Deeper tissue changes happen more slowly
Recommended

Maintenance plan

Consistency = real transformation.

  • ✓ Muscle tissue adapts and responds better over time
  • ✓ Reduces the risk of injury and chronic pain
  • ✓ Better posture, range of motion, and sleep quality
  • ✓ Each session builds on the last one
  • ✓ More cost-effective — prevention is cheaper than correction

Recommended frequency

Find your ideal rhythm

There is no single right answer. Your ideal frequency depends on your goals, activity level, and how well you listen to your body.

General maintenance

Once a month

The basic level of preventive care. It keeps your body balanced and helps prevent tension from building up.

  • Gradually reduces chronic tension
  • Improves mood and sleep
  • Ideal for building a healthy habit
Best for: People with moderately active lifestyles, office work, or those just getting started.

⭐ Most recommended

Every 2–3 weeks

The sweet spot for most people. Your body keeps the benefits between sessions and progress builds over time.

  • Visible improvements in posture and mobility
  • Faster muscle recovery
  • Provides sustained chronic pain relief
  • Each session goes deeper
Best for: Recurrent neck or back pain, active people, high stress levels, and athletes in training.

Intensive / recovery

1–2 times per week

For specific conditions or active recovery. A higher therapeutic dose for a defined period of time.

  • Post-injury or post-surgery recovery
  • Athletes preparing for competition
  • Management of acute pain or fibromyalgia
Best for: Rehabilitation, intense training periods, and chronic conditions under supervision.

What you can expect

Results by stage

With a consistent maintenance plan (every 2–3 weeks), this is how your body typically progresses:

1

First session

Your body releases surface tension. You feel immediate relief, better circulation, and mental calm. You may feel a little soreness the next day — that is normal and it passes quickly.

Immediate relief
M1

First month (2–3 sessions)

Muscle tissue begins to release chronic patterns. You notice less tension in problem areas, better sleep, and improvements in posture.

Early changes
M3

Three months (6–8 sessions)

Changes become more noticeable. You gain more range of motion, chronic pain decreases significantly, and performance improves at the gym or at work. Your therapist can work more deeply in each session.

Visible transformation
6+

Six months and beyond

Massage becomes active prevention. Your body maintains the benefits between sessions, the risk of injury decreases, and wellness becomes a constant state — not something you have to recover.
